You may opt in to receive text messages from Select Source Water by:

By opting in through any of the above methods, you expressly consent to receive automated text messages from Select Source Water at the mobile number you provided. You confirm that you are the owner or authorized user of the mobile device associated with that number.

Consent to receive text messages is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services from Select Source Water. You may schedule a water test, purchase a system, or use any of our services without opting in to text messages.

2. Types of Messages

If you opt in, you may receive the following types of text messages:

  • Appointment Confirmations: Confirmation that your free water test or service appointment has been scheduled
  • Appointment Reminders: Reminders before your scheduled appointment (typically 24 hours and 1 hour prior)
  • Service Updates: Updates related to your installation, system performance, or maintenance schedule
  • Follow-Up Messages: Post-appointment or post-installation follow-up communications
